About We Care Paws

Founded on a love for NYC pets and a commitment to exceptional care

Our Story

We Care Paws was founded in 2020 by a group of pet lovers who saw a need for truly personalized, premium pet care in Manhattan. Located near the iconic Washington Square Park, we serve the Greenwich Village community and surrounding neighborhoods.

What started as a small dog walking service has grown into a comprehensive pet care company offering everything from daily walks to overnight sitting and grooming services. But no matter how much we've grown, our core mission remains the same: treating every pet like family.

We believe that pets deserve more than just basic care—they deserve adventures, attention, and genuine love. That's why we hire only the most passionate animal lovers and invest heavily in training, safety, and technology to give you complete peace of mind.

Proudly serving Greenwich Village since 2020
