Our Services

From daily walks to overnight care, we provide comprehensive pet services designed with love and expertise.

Dog Walking

Adventures in Greenwich Village

Individual or group walks tailored to your dog's energy level. We explore Washington Square Park, Hudson River Park, and local neighborhood spots.

  • 30-minute or 60-minute sessions
  • GPS tracking for every walk
  • Photo updates sent to you
  • Flexible scheduling

In-Home Sitting

Comfort of Their Own Space

Overnight care so your pet stays in their comfortable environment while you're away. We maintain their routine and provide lots of love and attention.

  • Overnight stays at your home
  • Feeding and medication
  • Daily updates with photos
  • Home security check

Pet Spa & Grooming

Pamper Your Pup

High-end bathing, nail trimming, and 'paw-dicures' to keep your pet looking and feeling their best. We use all-natural, pet-safe products.

  • Full grooming packages
  • Nail trimming & filing
  • Ear cleaning
  • Specialty shampoos available

Puppy Socialization

Building Confidence Early

Special classes for young pups to learn manners and play with other puppies in a safe, supervised environment. Perfect for puppies 8-16 weeks old.

  • Age-appropriate play groups
  • Basic obedience training
  • Positive reinforcement methods
  • Small class sizes (max 6 pups)
