Cancellation Policy

We understand that plans change. Cancellations made 24 hours or more in advance will receive a full refund. Cancellations made less than 24 hours before the scheduled service will be charged 50% of the service fee. Same-day cancellations or no-shows will be charged the full service fee.

Pet Vaccination Requirements

For the safety of all pets in our care, we require that all dogs and cats be up-to-date on the following vaccinations:

  • Dogs: Rabies, DHPP (Distemper, Hepatitis, Parainfluenza, Parvovirus), Bordetella (kennel cough)
  • Cats: Rabies, FVRCP (Feline Viral Rhinotracheitis, Calicivirus, Panleukopenia)

Proof of vaccination must be provided before the first service. Pets that are not up-to-date on vaccinations will not be accepted into our care.

Emergency Medical Authorization

By using our services, you authorize We Care Paws to seek emergency veterinary care for your pet if needed. We will make every effort to contact you first, but in the event of a medical emergency where immediate care is required, we are authorized to transport your pet to the nearest veterinary facility and approve necessary treatment. You agree to reimburse We Care Paws for any veterinary expenses incurred on behalf of your pet.

Liability & Insurance

We Care Paws carries comprehensive liability insurance and is bonded for your protection. While we take every precaution to ensure your pet's safety, pet ownership involves inherent risks. By using our services, you acknowledge these risks and agree that We Care Paws is not liable for injuries or incidents that occur despite our reasonable care and supervision, except in cases of gross negligence or willful misconduct.

Privacy Policy

Information We Collect

To provide our services, we collect the following information:

  • Your name, address, phone number, and email address
  • Emergency contact information
  • Pet information (name, breed, age, medical history, behavior notes)
  • Home access information (keys, security codes, gate codes)
  • Payment information
  • Photos and videos taken during pet care services

How We Use Your Information

We use your information solely to provide and improve our pet care services. This includes scheduling appointments, accessing your home for pet care, communicating with you about your pet, processing payments, and sending you photos and updates. We may also use your information to send service reminders and promotional offers (you can opt out at any time).

Data Sharing

We do not sell, rent, or share your personal information with third parties except as necessary to provide our services (e.g., payment processing) or as required by law. Photos of your pet may be shared on our social media with your explicit permission only.
